Here at Temple Inn & Suites our goal is to make your next visit to the Temple as comfortable as possible. We take care of all of your needs so you can focus on making your experience joyful and spiritually fulfilling.

Operating in tandem with the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints we are able to offer specialized services at our locations around the world. If you are not a member of the Church, we hope you will still feel welcome to enjoy many of the services we offer. All we ask is that you abide by the standards of the Church.

Please fell free to peruse some of the services and temples we have featured on our home page, and explore the rest of the site for more detailed information.
