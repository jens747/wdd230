The Perfect Reception

We'll handle the details

You've been planning for this moment all of your life. We want to help you make it as unforgetable as the love you have for your eternal companion.

With all the amenities you will need for the perfect wedding reception, while also offering locations that are within walking distance of the Temple, we provide an array of resources that will help you make your wedding an elegant and enjoyable experience for you and your guests.

Reception Hall

We offer several spacious reception halls at each of our locations. Each hall provides kitchen access for food preparation, as well as a customizable menu and theme based meals for reception attendees.

Elegant Table Settings

We have staff on-hand to assist with setup, food preparation, serving, and clean-up.

However, as per your preference, full catering options are also offered.

Ceremonies

For family members that are not able to attend the Temple, chapels and other rooms are available for ring ceremonies and the exchanging of wedding vows.
