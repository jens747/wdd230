The Traveler's Oasis

For full-time Temple missionaries, as well as patrons who may be traveling away from home for a prolonged period of time, the Temple Inn & Suites offers options for extended stay. These options include features that evoke all of the comforts of home without actually being there.

We offer several different styles of rooms in our extended stay apartments. Options range from one to three bedrooms, single and double bathrooms, dining area, study area, lounge area, washer and dryer, kitchenette with sink, stove, and dishwasher, and much more.

If you are looking for accommodations for an extended period of time, we hope that you will view what we have to offer below, and consider the Temple Inn & Suites as your temporary home away from home while you selflessly serve the Lord in your calling.
