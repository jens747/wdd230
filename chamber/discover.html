Discover Yellowstone

A Hidden Gem

Nestled between the Sierra Nevada and the Rocky Mountains is the scenic town of Yellowstone. Visitors can experience this gorgeous birdseye view first-hand by riding the Miner's Cove Escalator.

Take In The View

Silver Stream Lake is just one of the many lakes, rivers, and streams that surround Yellowstone. Silver Stream is fed by a fresh water spring and is open to fishers year-round to test their rod and reel against our local bass, trout, and perch. Interested parties can visit Guppy's Bait & Tackle to see if they can best the record catch, a 270lb. giant pulled from Silver Stream Lake back on Feb. 22, 1903.

At Your Leisure

If you're into country music, line-dancing, bar-fighting, and beer-drinking, you'll find it here at the Cowboy - all except the bar-fighting. The Cowboy is one of the many fine establishments we have here in Yellowstone that offer up the best entertainment, food, and drinks you'll find in the state. It's good, clean fun for family members of all ages.

Step Into The Past

Yellowstone is also home to a bevy of markets, boutiques, and thrift shops. We're extremely proud of our selection of antiquities establishments located on Oak Creek Crescent Street. The shops there boast a combined selection of over 10,000 antique items. You may even find that treasured miner's cap you've been hunting for these past 18 years.

Fresh Locally Grown Produce

For the best and freshest food that Yellowstone has to offer visit the Market Square Farmer's Market. All of the produce you'll find there is locally grown and picked fresh at peak ripeness.

Take Up A New Hobby

If you're looking to try something a little off the beaten path, try taking up a new hobby like knife and tomahawk throwing at Wicked Bill's Weaponry. They offer demonstrations and classes daily at 9:00 and 11:00 in the morning and 3:30 and 5:30 in the afternoon. If Bill's is a little too wicked for you, and who can blame you, we also have classes for yoga, cycling, pottery, Tai-Chi, meditaion, and basketry.

Exotic Local Wildlife

We hope you've had fun here in Yellowstone, but before you leave please book a tour to traverse our scenic landscape and witness the local exotic wildlife. Yellowstone is home to some of the most amazing fauna that North America has to offer including bison, bear, wolves, deer, and bald eagles.
